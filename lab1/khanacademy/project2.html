Recipe #1 Sushi

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Sushi Rice 2 cups
Rice Vinegar 1/4 cup
Nori (Seaweed Sheets) 4 sheets
Fish (Salmon/Tuna) 200g
Vegetables (Cucumber, Avocado) As needed

Step 1: Cook sushi rice and mix with rice vinegar. Let it cool to room temperature.

Step 2: Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo mat. Spread rice evenly, then add fish and vegetables. Roll tightly and cut into bite-sized pieces.

Recipe #2 Pizza

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza Dough 1 base
Tomato Sauce 1/2 cup
Mozzarella Cheese 2 cups
Pepperoni (or any toppings) As desired

Step 1: Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Roll out pizza dough on a baking sheet.

Step 2: Spread tomato sauce on the dough, add mozzarella cheese, and top with pepperoni. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
